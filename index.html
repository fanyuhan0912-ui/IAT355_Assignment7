Mapping the
Electric Vehicle charging landscape

This project focuses on how expanding public charging infrastructure in major Canadian cities, particularly Vancouver, can accelerate the adoption of electric vehicles (EVs). By visualizing EV registration data and the distribution of charging stations, this project highlights the relationship between infrastructure investment and EV usage growth.

What you can explore on this website

The website is structured around a small set of focused visualizations: a map of charging station density, a bar chart comparing major cities, and a simple timeline of EV growth. In Milestone 2, these visualizations are working prototypes; styling and advanced interaction will be refined in later milestones.
